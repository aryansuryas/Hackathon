# Perfect Hackathon / Pitchathon PPT Structure

## Slide 1: Title Slide
- Startup/App Name
- Tagline
- Team Members
- College Name

## Slide 2: Problem Statement
- What is the problem?
- Why is it important?
- Real-world statistics
- Emotional impact

## Slide 3: Existing Solution Gaps
- Why current systems fail
- Delayed response
- No AI prediction
- Poor communication
- No offline support

## Slide 4: Your Solution
- Product/App overview
- Problem-solving approach
- Main innovation

## Slide 5: How It Works
- Workflow diagram
- Architecture
- AI process
- APIs/Sensors used

### Example Flow
```
User
 ↓
AI Detection
 ↓
Emergency Alert
 ↓
Rescue Coordination
```

## Slide 6: Key Features
- AI-based prediction
- SOS emergency mode
- Offline alerts
- Drone integration
- Multi-language support
- Real-time tracking

## Slide 7: Market Opportunity
- Market size
- Target audience
- Industry growth
- Potential users

## Slide 8: Business Model
- Government partnerships
- Subscription plans
- SaaS model
- NGO collaborations
- API licensing

## Slide 9: Competitor Analysis
Compare your solution with existing products.

## Slide 10: Demo / Prototype ⚠️
Most Important Slide

Include:
- App screenshots
- UI mockups
- Prototype
- Live demo

## Slide 11: Impact
- Social impact
- Economic impact
- Lives saved
- Sustainability benefits

## Slide 12: Future Roadmap
- Expansion plans
- Future AI features
- Government partnerships
- Scaling vision

## Slide 13: Thank You / Q&A

## Hackathon PPT

## Pitchathon / Startup PPT

- Methodology

- Clarity
